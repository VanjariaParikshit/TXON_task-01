About Us

We are a PG accommodation provider offering comfortable and affordable accommodation to students and working professionals. Our facilities include spacious rooms, clean washrooms, and a common kitchen. We also provide 24/7 security and on-call maintenance services.

Rooms

We offer different types of rooms to suit your needs and budget. Our rooms are spacious, well-ventilated, and furnished with essential amenities such as a bed, a wardrobe, and a study table. You can choose from single or shared rooms.
